Employers

  • 2 to 4 weeks of remedial onboarding per hire
  • Language barrier with every Saudi customer
  • 40% turnover within 90 days for untrained staff
  • Inconsistent service quality across every shift
  • No way to verify skills before the worker arrives
SAR 1,500+ onboarding cost per hire
Workers

No preparation, no leverage, no path

  • Arrive with no formal café or hospitality skills
  • Cannot speak Arabic or understand Saudi culture
  • No certification means lower starting wage
  • No career progression without recognised credentials
  • Vulnerable without documentation and proof of skill
Earn 50 to 100% less than certified peers

The Solution

Certified before they board.
Ready on Day 1.

SkillBridge Arabia is a certified digital training platform for frontline F&B workers. They complete training on their phone in their home country before their visa is stamped.

Workers arrive certified, Arabic-speaking and SFDA-compliant. Employers see the certificate before the interview. Agencies track progress in a live dashboard.

1

Agency enrols the worker in 2 minutes

Name, email, target role. Worker gets a login link. Agency dashboard activates.

2

Worker trains on their phone at home

30 hours, their own language, their own pace. Agency sees progress in real time.

3

QR certificate generated on exam pass

Employer scans and verifies in seconds. Candidate starts Day 1 ready.
