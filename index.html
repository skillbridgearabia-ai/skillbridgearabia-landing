The Process

Three steps from home
to certified

The agency enrols the worker. The worker trains at home on their phone. The employer receives a certified candidate before the visa is stamped.

01 / Agency
📋

Agency enrols the worker

The recruitment agency adds the candidate to their SkillBridge dashboard with a name, email and target role. The worker receives a login link immediately. Two minutes per worker. Progress is visible in real time.

Two minutes to enrol
02 / Worker
📱

Worker trains on their phone

Thirty hours of video lessons, quizzes and language modules at their own pace, in their own language, at home before departure. The agency and employer see live progress throughout.

Typically two to four weeks
03 / Employer
🏆

Certified candidate arrives

The worker passes the final exam. A QR-verified certificate is generated instantly. The employer verifies it in seconds. The worker starts on Day 1 skilled, SFDA-compliant and with working Arabic.

Certificate instant on pass
